iPOD PLAYER

The iPod Classic emerged during a transformative era for Apple, fondly called the iYears. It was more than just a music player. The iPod Classic changed how we experienced music.

First released in 2001, it went through six generations before the final model arrived in 2007 with 160 GB of space and the distinctive click wheel. It was discontinued in 2014, but its influence has never really faded. People still hold onto it for more than its storage or design; it carries a certain technostalgia about it.
